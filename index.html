where real world experience meets limitless possibilities

Hi. My name is Evan Aubry and I'm a landscape architect. For my job, I get to design really cool places, from small playgrounds and plazas to gigantic shopping complexes and even huge vacation resorts!

In my free time, I like to use my creativity to design landscapes and settings that aren't limited by constraints of the real world. And that's why I've gotten into video game design - it's what I do already, but I can really let my imagination run wild.

I've taken up video game design as a hobby for about 2 years now, and have taught myself just about everything I know about programming and using various forms of design media. A lot of these projects are just in their first stages, but I'm not giving up anytime soon because this is something that I'm incredibly passionate about.
